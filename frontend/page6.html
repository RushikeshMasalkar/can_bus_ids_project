PRIVACY FIRST

Data Handling & Provenance

We treat vehicle data with the same sensitivity as financial records. Our Data Minimization Policy ensures that only essential CAN message IDs and payloads required for anomaly detection are ever transmitted to our analysis engines.

Anonymization

VIN and GPS coordinates are masked at the edge before analysis, ensuring data remains non-identifiable during processing.

Provenance

We strictly utilize public research datasets (CAR-HACK, OTIDS) and verified OEM captures with documented lineage.
